Which calendars does Until use?

By default, Until reads from all your calendars. To choose specific ones, click the Until icon in your menu bar, open Settings (or press Cmd+,), and select which calendars to include.

This means Until works with anything that shows up in Apple's Calendar app — iCloud, Google Calendar, Exchange, Outlook, etc.
The app icon doesn't appear in the Dock.

That's intentional. Until lives only in your menu bar so it stays out of your way.

How do I quit the app?

Click the Until icon in your menu bar, then click Quit in the popover.

How do I open settings?

Click the Until icon, then click the gear icon, or press Cmd+, while the popover is open.

I want to hide a specific event from the countdown.

Open the popover, hover over the event you want to hide, and click the eye-slash icon. You can clear all hidden events from Settings → Ignored Events.

Does Until work offline?

Yes. Until only reads your local calendar, so it works without an internet connection.

Does Until send my data anywhere?

No. See the privacy policy for details. All calendar reading happens on your Mac.
