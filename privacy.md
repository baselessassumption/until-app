Privacy Policy
Last updated: 20 May 2026
Until is a macOS menu bar app that displays a countdown to your next calendar event.
What we collect
Nothing.
Until does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on any server. There is no account, no login, and no sync.
How your calendar data is used
Until reads events from your local calendar (via Apple's EventKit framework) only to display a countdown in your menu bar. This reading happens entirely on your Mac. Your calendar data is never sent to us or to any third party.
If you revoke calendar access in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Calendars, Until will stop reading your events immediately.
Analytics and tracking
We do not use analytics services, crash reporting services, advertising identifiers, or any third-party tracking.
Children
Until is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect any information from anyone.
Changes to this policy
If this policy changes in a future version, we will update this page and note the change in the app's release notes on the Mac App Store.
